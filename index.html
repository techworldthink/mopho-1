About

MOPHO

Mopho is dedicated to producing handmade cakes & chocolates using traditional baking techniques. we bake with whole grains and go through a slow proofing process, which sharpens and improves the taste. MOPHO never compromise on the ingredients of its baked goods, which is why you can consume them everyday and never tire of it. We select only the finest ingredients. Mopho bakes both sweet and savory items including cakes, pastries, pizza and our famous chocolates.. MOPHO has been serving the locals its cherished creations for over 2 years. We wish to pass on our legacy to the rest of the country.
